Salesian Commitment




Church



Salesian is a church because of the religious ways and traditions that we follow. Salesian is always following in the footsteps of St. John Bosco. Salesian has their students pray and practice Catholicism every day, so their students can have a good relationship with God.




School



Salesian is a school because of how dedicated both the teachers and students are. The teachers dedicate themselves to teaching the students, so that they can learn new things, prepare for college, and prepare for life.




Playground



Salesian is a playground because it is a place where you can always have fun. Salesian is a place where you can participate and compete in several sports.




Home



Salesian is a home because it is a place where all people are welcome. The brotherhood at Salesian makes everyone feel at home and comfortable.

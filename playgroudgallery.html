Why I think Salesian is a Playground?

Salesian is a playground for me because it is a place where I have fun with my Salesian brothers. At Salesian we enjoy playing sports during break and playing sports competitively. We enjoy playing during break on the field, playing and messing around. Everyone at Salesian is able to have fun with their friends, and have fun during school events. As Salesian brothers we learn to bond at the rallies and school activities. It was very fun when I would play warball and indoor soccer with my classmates, during P.E. my freshman year. This is why I think Salesian is a playground.
